The alex brady Course in Miracles is a set of steps with the purpose of taking the learner from where they are to personal spiritual enlightenment. These steps are heavily based on the teaching found within the book A Course in Miracles.

Spiritual discovery is a personal process. abcim is undertaken as a self-guided process. Intrapersonal (within-yourself) contemplation and examination of the teachings is encouraged and fundamentally a required aspect of this course. If you prefer to learn in solitude, that is fully acceptable. The goal of Higher-Self realization is flexible to any lifestyle.

We provide both ACIM and non-ACIM resources. We also provide an online community for Self-seekers to share and socialize.
